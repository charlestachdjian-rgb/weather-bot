Feb 15 and Feb 18 both had late-evening temperature surges that broke the Ceiling NO and Locked-In YES strategies. Could SYNOP or Open-Meteo have warned us?

Conclusion: Add a Trend Safety Check

On both losing days, at least one data source was already showing a rising temperature trend at 4-5pm — the exact time our risky signals fired.

A simple safeguard would prevent these losses:

if trend_at_signal_time == RISING on ANY source:
    → do NOT fire Ceiling NO or Locked-In YES

This would have saved all 4 losing trades (-$400) while only blocking signals that were going to lose anyway. The Floor NO strategy (T1 + T2) doesn't need this check — it's already mathematically safe.
