The Safe Playbook (Validated on 19 Days)

Strategy 1: Guaranteed Floor NO (Risk: ~Zero)

  • Rule: Once METAR/WU shows a new daily high of X, every bracket below X must resolve NO
  • When: As soon as the high is recorded — often by late morning
  • Edge: Small ($2-5 per $100) — these brackets are usually already repriced, but laggards exist
  • Track record: 100% win rate across all 19 days. Temperature cannot un-reach a high.
  • Capital needed: Low. These are small guaranteed edges on illiquid brackets

Strategy 2: Late-Day Ceiling NO (Risk: Very Low)

  • Rule: After 16:00 local time, buy NO on all brackets 2+ degrees above the daily high
  • Validation: On 16/19 days, the market was still repricing at 15:00+. Temperature almost never jumps 2+ degrees in the final hours of a winter day.
  • Edge: $3-10 per $100. These brackets still trade at 3-10% YES due to residual uncertainty
  • Exception: Warm fronts (Feb 21 Paris: jumped 3°C in final hours). Use SYNOP 0.1°C + Open-Meteo trend to detect

Strategy 3: Adjacent Bracket NO (Risk: Low-Moderate, Best Risk/Reward)

  • Rule: When one bracket is clearly leading (>60% YES), buy NO on the bracket(s) on the wrong side
  • Example: If 38-39°F is at 60% and the daily high is already 38°F, then 36-37°F cannot win anymore — buy NO
  • Validation: 20 opportunities across 19 days with peak YES > 40% (1.1/day)
  • Edge: $20-97 per $100 — these are the biggest edges in the entire market
  • Timing: Best in the afternoon (12:00-17:00) when the daily high starts to stabilize
  • Use SYNOP 0.1°C: If bracket boundary is at 14°C and SYNOP reads 14.8°C, the high has already crossed. The adjacent lower bracket (13°C) is dead even if METAR still shows 14°C

What NOT To Do

  • Don't buy YES early in the day — the winning bracket often starts below 30% YES and doesn't lock in until 15:00-17:00. You'd be gambling, not trading.
  • Don't trust Open-Meteo for absolute temps — it reads 0.8°C below WU consistently. Only use it for trend direction.
  • Don't assume SYNOP = WU — they disagreed on 2 of 8 Paris days. WU can report a brief spike that SYNOP's hourly data misses.
  • Don't fade the adjacent bracket too early — on Feb 15 NYC, 36-37°F hit 97% YES at 16:00 before collapsing. It looked like the winner until the very end.
  • Size small — daily volume per bracket is $5-50K (Paris) or $15-80K (NYC). Large orders will move the market against you.
