Strategy Guide

Floor NO — Tier 1 (green)
Fires the instant METAR shows a new running high that crosses bracket_top + 0.5°C. Zero risk — a temperature can never un-reach a high. We buy NO on that bracket.

Floor NO — Tier 2 (yellow)
Fires once at 9am. If the forecast high minus bracket top ≥ 4.0°C, we call the bracket dead early. Very high confidence, but carries small forecast risk.

Ceiling NO (red)
After 16:00 CET, brackets whose floor is ≥ 2.0°C above the running daily high are unreachable. The day is cooling and can't climb that much. We buy NO. Requires YES ≥ 3% to be worth it.

Locked-In YES (blue)
After 17:00 CET, the daily high is essentially locked. If it rounds to a bracket and the market still prices YES below 80%, we buy YES before the market catches up.
