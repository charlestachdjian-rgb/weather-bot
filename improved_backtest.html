The 5 Safeguards

1
Open-Meteo Peak Hour
If OM forecasts the daily max coming AFTER our signal time → block. On the losing days, OM showed peak at 10-11pm.
2
Open-Meteo Remaining Max
If OM forecasts higher temperatures in the remaining hours of the day than the current running high → block.
3
Open-Meteo Forecast vs Bracket
If OM's daily forecast high (+ bias) is within 1°C of the bracket floor → block. The temperature might actually reach the bracket.
4
Multi-Source Trend
If ANY source (METAR, SYNOP, Open-Meteo) shows a rising trend over the last 3 hours → block. Temperature hasn't peaked.
5
SYNOP Velocity
If SYNOP (0.1°C precision) rose more than 0.3°C in the last 3 hours → block. Catches subtle upward drift.
